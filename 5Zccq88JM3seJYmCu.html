About the Game

Crystal Legends is an epic mobile RPG that combines strategic gameplay with stunning visuals. Embark on an extraordinary journey through a mystical realm where powerful crystals hold the key to ultimate power.

Features

  • Collect and evolve unique heroes
  • Strategic turn-based combat
  • PvP arena battles
  • Guild warfare
  • Regular events and updates

Heroes

In Crystal Legends, heroes are the backbone of your journey. Each hero is unique, with special abilities and traits that bring something valuable to your team. From powerful Paladins to enigmatic Sorcerers, you'll encounter a wide array of legendary figures, each with their own background and strengths.

Heroes of Crystal Legends

In Crystal Legends, heroes are the backbone of your journey. Each hero is unique, with special abilities and traits that bring something valuable to your team. From powerful Paladins to enigmatic Sorcerers, you'll encounter a wide array of legendary figures, each with their own background and strengths.

Hero Details

Name: Captain

Tier: T3

Abilities

Spectral Ship

Summons a ship to charge toward enemies, dealing Magic Damage and Stunning them.

  • LV 1: Sends allies and captain a rum boost, garanting them 20% final physical.
  • LV 2: Increases Damage dealt to 4.9 Magic Intensity.
  • LV 3: Extends Stun duration to 5 seconds.

Flood Surge

Summons flood to attack a random enemy, dealing Magic Damage, inflicting Knockup and Stun.

  • LV 1: Increases Damage dealt to 1.4 Magic Intensity.
  • LV 2: Extends Stun duration to 3 seconds.
  • LV 3: Increases Damage dealt to 1.6 Magic Intensity.

Aquatic Blade

Wields the blade, dealing Physical Damage to enemies in a small range.

  • LV 1: Reduces Move Speed and Haste of enemies hit by Aquatic Blade by 25 for 3 seconds.
  • LV 2: Increases Damage dealt to 1.2 Physical Attack.
  • LV 3: Increases Damage dealt to 1.4 Physical Attack.

Empowered Force

Increases Strength.

  • LV 1: Increases Strength by 15.
  • LV 2: Increases Strength by 25.
  • LV 3: Increases Strength by 40.
